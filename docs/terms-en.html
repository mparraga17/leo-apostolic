2. Purpose and description of the Application

Leo Apostolic is a Catholic devotional application designed as a permanent spiritual companion, also marking the visit of Pope Leo XIV to Madrid in June 2026. The Application offers:

  • "Today" screen with the saint of the day, a quote from the Pope, and the daily prayer.
  • Collection of traditional prayers in Spanish and Latin (public domain).
  • Traditional liturgical hymnal (public domain) with lyrics and Latin versions.
  • Links to contemporary religious music on third-party platforms (Spotify).
  • Informational schedule of the Pope's public events, with times and locations.
  • Curated selection of cultural-religious places in Madrid and its region.
  • Section of products linked to third-party affiliate programs.
  • Local notifications reminding of public events.
